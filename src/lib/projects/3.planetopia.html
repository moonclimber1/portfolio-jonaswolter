Museum Exhibition

For the current temporary exhibition at the Museum of Communication, Berne, I designed and programmed 12 touch screen terminals with a quiz on sustainability and subsequent evaluation. Based on their answers, visitors could sign a suitable world change agreement at the final stage. I also designed and programmed software for a detailed anonymous evaluation of visitor behaviour in the exhibition, which gave the marketing department and museum management valuable new insights.
