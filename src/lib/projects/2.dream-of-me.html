Dream of Me

Audiovisual Performance

We will flirt, slowly approach each other, touch each other gently, kiss passionately, get lost in each other, feel passionate and alive, caress each other softly, and then just dream. Dream of Me combines experimental sounds with dynamic visuals and takes you on an immersive journey into the depths of a sensual world.
