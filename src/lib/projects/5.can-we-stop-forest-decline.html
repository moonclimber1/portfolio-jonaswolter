Can we stop forest decline?

Data Visualization

For the SDGViz Award of the Swiss Federal Statistical Office, I developed a new type of data visualisation that shows the development of global forest areas over the last 20 years. The infographics were generated in P5.js with a specially developed algorithm for balancing the curve areas.
